Welcome to our Corgi Force Community website

We started off as an in-house team that eventually started its own community and separated from the community it started in. We have since grown a lot and our community is open for both competitive and casual players in Overwatch.

The community allows for people to create a team without the hassle of setting up their server, searching scrims on various discords, and no real community feeling without having to manage multiple teams in the server. In our community, we take this all away, and help you with what’s really important: Your team. You manage your team, we do the rest. We set up your team in our server based on your name and color for the team, and all teams will receive artwork (Logo and banner) if they’ve settled and have been around for more than a week.

Corgi Force houses several teams in the community, and there are no restrictions on the amount of teams that can be created, on the SR the team is, and how many members you wish to have. Team Captains are able to micro-manage their own category in the Discord server and the only time you’ll be asking a Community Staff to do something for you is to take, or give the team role to someone you invited.
